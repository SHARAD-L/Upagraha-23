Bitflow

Description

BitFlow is an exhilarating opportunity for digital design enthusiasts to showcase their knowledge and creativity in the world of digital circuits. Dive into the world of logic gates, flip-flops, and microcontrollers as you compete in a challenging event that will put your design and problem-solving skills to the test.
BitFlow is your chance to demonstrate your expertise in digital design and circuitry. Join us at Upagraha'23 and let your knowledge and creativity shine as you compete for the BitFlow champion title!

Round 1  MCQ QUIZ

The competition kicks off with a multiple-choice question (MCQ) quiz that evaluates your knowledge of digital design principles, logic gates, and circuit analysis.

Round 2  COMPONENT DESIGN

Participants who excel in the MCQ round will advance to the second round, where they'll have the chance to apply their skills by designing digital components using a designated digital circuit simulator.

Rules

  • Team of three members.
  • Detailed instructions for Round 2 will be provided on the day of the event.
  • Any form of cheating or misconduct will result in disqualification.
  • Participants are only allowed to use the specified simulator provided for the competition.
  • Prizes will be awarded to the top-performing teams based on their performance in both rounds.
  • The decision of judges will be final and violation of rules can lead to disqualification.
